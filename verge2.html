Gameplay üéÆ

- Everyone starts with 7 lives and starts as a green player.
- Your goal in the series is to try to not lose lives.
- Lives can be transferred from one player to another by using the command /givelife PlayerName 
Keep in mind that you are transferring lives (you will lose your life and it will be given to the player you choose)
- We prefer everyone to use the Ingame Voice Chat or the designated voice channels on AMAZING FRIENDS Discord

MEANING OF THE COLORS ON THE NAMES

Red Player : Player has 1 life left (player is on their last life) 
A red player's goal is to kill and make more players to become red lived, at the same time, 
they need to cautious to not lose their last life. If they die, they are out of the series. They become spectators.

Yellow Player: Player has 2 lives left, they should not attempt to kill any Green  lived or Yellow  lived players. 
They can however attack any player as self defense ONLY IF THE OTHER PLAYER TRIES TO KILL
They can kill a Red player at any time.

Green Player: Player has 3 or more lives left, they should not attempt to kill any Green lived or Yellow lived players.
They can however attack any player as self defense ONLY IF THE OTHER PLAYER TRIES TO KILL
They can kill a Red player at any time.

ASSASSIN

- At the beginning of Every session an ASSASSIN is chosen by the server randomly, and the assassins goal for the session is to kill 1 player (who is having atleast more than 1 life, ie. Assassin CANT KILL A RED PLAYER!)
- The ASSASSIN should try to keep their identity as an Assassin a secret from everyone (Just like an imposter in Among Us)
